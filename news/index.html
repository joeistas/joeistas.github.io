  • Slice Keyboard: Touch typing on a touch screen

    Dec 3, 2012

    The Slice Keyboard is an innovative touch keyboard design that makes it possible to touch type on a touch screen. Slice's main innovation is a blank home row where you place your fingers. Then keys are shown depending on which fingers are placed on the home row. Each key is in a direction relative to the keys on the home row. This allows you to type without having to look at the keys. The home row positions can statically placed or adjust to your finger placement.
