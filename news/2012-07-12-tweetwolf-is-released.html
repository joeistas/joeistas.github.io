Tweetwolf is Released

Jul 11, 2012

We have just released a new application for Android called TweetWolf. TweetWolf lets you play the party game Werewolf on Twitter. You can play with all of your twitter followers without having to sign up for another service. Also, your followers don't even need the app to play, they don't even need a smartphone if they have access to the Twitter website or a phone that can receive text messages. Players only need a Twitter account to join or even host a game.
