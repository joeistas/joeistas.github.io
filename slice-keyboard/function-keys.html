Function Keys

Function keys are on the left and right sides of the keyboard. They are designed to be used with your little finger and thumb.

Space Bar

On Two Hand Layouts the space bar is at the bottom center of the display.

Hand Switch

On single hand layouts a hand switch button is at the bottom center of the screen. The Hand Switch button swaps the keyboard layout between use with the Left and Right hands.
